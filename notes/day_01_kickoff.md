## Research Question

What does the current open-source ecosystem for consumer tax bookkeeping and US tax-return preparation/submission look like, and how feasible is it to connect representative tools to other software through APIs, CLIs, file formats, libraries, schemas, or comparable programmatic surfaces?

## Final Deliverables

- Written research report with landscape overview, per-tool findings, cross-tool comparison, and integration recommendations.
- Prototype integration using synthetic data only.
- 20-30 minute presentation deck covering the research method, findings, prototype demo, recommendation, and next steps.

- Use synthetic data only throughout the project.
- Evaluate tools with a repeatable rubric so the final recommendation is evidence-backed.
